Roads and safety

Roads and safety

France has an excellent road and motorway network.

Speed limit on motorways is 130 Km/H (110 Km/H. in rain and on divided highways)

Speed limit on main roads is 90 Km/H and 50 Km/H in built-up areas. (unless otherwise indicated.)
